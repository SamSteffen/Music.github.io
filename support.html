Support Sam Steffen...

If you like what Sam's doing and want to support him, his music is available for listening, purchase and download on Bandcamp.*

If you like what Sam's doing and want to support him, but don't feel that a financial contribution is in the cards for you, there are other ways you can show your love:

  • Follow, subscribe, like and share Sam Steffen on any or all of these platforms:
    • AppleMusic
    • Bandcamp
    • Instagram
    • Spotify
    • Soundcloud
    • YouTube
  • Come hear Sam Steffen live at his next show.
  • Register to vote, if you haven't already.
  • (Fellow musicians) Learn to play some of his songs, and play them at your next gig.
  • (Homeowners, -dwellers) Contact Sam Steffen and see if he'll play your next houseshow!
  • (Venue owners, managers) Reach out to Sam Steffen and see if he's available to open or headline your next show!

* For every dollar that you spend on Bandcamp, the artist receives $0.75.
