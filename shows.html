Kaleido Arts Fest
The Carrot Community Arts Coffee House
9351 118 Ave NW
Edmonton, AB

Sam Steffen is joining the lineup at the 2024 Kaleido Arts Festival. He'll be playing Saturday, September 14 from 3 - 3:45 p.m. MT AND Sunday, September 15 from 1 - 1:45 p.m. MT at the Carrot Community Arts Coffee House, 9351 118 Ave NW, Edmonton, AB.

Kaleido is a family-friendly, joyous, exuberant, radically open arts festival that shares the love of the Alberta Avenue Arts District. Celebrate the arts and discover music, theatre, dance, street performances, food, artisans, art galleries, markets, and even more in alleys, rooftops, parks and on Alberta Avenue. Kaleido is run by the registered charity, Arts on the Ave. Our organization works to support community and promote the arts on 118th Ave and its surrounding neighbourhoods.

The Carrot Community Arts Coffeehouse is a volunteer-powered, Arts on the Ave established coffeehouse and art gallery in the Alberta Avenue community of Edmonton. In addition to serving world-class coffee and locally made goods, The Carrot showcases the works of local artisans, and has engaged and empowered artists and community members since 2007.

Wyte Avenue Art Walk
Whyte Avenue, between 103 & 107th Streets
Edmonton, AB

Sam Steffen's making his Edmonton debut this weekend at the Edmonton Whyte Avenue Art Walk in the Old Strathcona neighborhood. He'll be playing Saturday 12 July at "Dog Bark" on Whyte Ave and 106th St, from 2 - 2:45 p.m. MT; Sunday 13 July he'll be playing at the gazebo in Wilbert McIntyre Park, next to the Old Strathcona Farmer's Market, from 11:30 a.m. to Noon.

The Whyte Avenue Art Walk is Edmonton’s first Art Walk with 400 artists lining the streets of Old Strathcona during the second weekend in July to share their creative talents. People are invited to buy art directly from the artists and watch as they create. The Art Walk is a non-juried event that attracts professional artists, amateurs, and students.
