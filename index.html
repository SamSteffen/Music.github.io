Sam Steffen

Sam Steffen is a singer-songwriter currently based in Edmonton, Alberta whose songs are the torch-wood for a new generation of folk music that has learned from the best stuff in the tradition. Originally from Bethlehem, Pennsylvania, Sam's versatile musicianship, finger-picking, and storytelling has made him an outstanding voice in the Americana folk genre.
